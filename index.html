California

If you are sick of the cold and dreary winter weather, a trip to California is just what you need. The weather in California is almost always perfect, and you will be able to spend your days outdoors enjoying all that the state has to offer. From hiking and biking to surfing and sunbathing, there is no shortage of activities to keep you busy. And when the sun goes down, you can enjoy a perfect evening under the stars.

Africa

The most well-known mountain to climb in Africa is Kilimanjaro, which sits at 5895m. It's one of the World's Seven Summits, and probably the easiest and undemanding to climb of them all. But there is also the High Atlas Mountains in Morocco, home to Mount Toubkal (4167m), Ouanoukrim (4088m) and Mount Mgoun (4071m). You can climb all three on our High Atlas Trekking holiday.
